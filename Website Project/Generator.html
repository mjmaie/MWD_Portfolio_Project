How does the Food Plan Generator work?

Simple! All you have to do is take a brief survey for some important information about you, and the website does the rest! You don’t have to enter anything too personal. Just your gender, the age range you fall into, and how active you’d say you are. Using that information, we’ll calculate a meal plan consisting of breakfast, lunch and dinner plans to last seven days. Each meal plan is carefully selected to include multiple food groups, and will have no more and no less than your precise calorie requirements.
